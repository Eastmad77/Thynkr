What is Thynkr?

Thynkr is a daily mental-fitness game. Play short, focused sets that challenge memory, logic, and attention — and build a streak over time.

How it works

  • Progress through three levels per set (12 questions each).
  • Redemption streaks get tougher by level (3 → 4 → 5 correct in a row).
  • Ad intermissions on free; ad-free on Pro.
